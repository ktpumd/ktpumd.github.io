Kappa Theta Pi

About Us

Kappa Theta Pi is the University of Maryland's first and only co-ed Professional Technology Fraternity. We help our members develop their technical and professional skills while forming a passionate community of individuals who share common interests.

History

Kappa Theta Pi was originally founded at the University of Michigan in January of 2012. The Epsilon Chapter of Kappa Theta Pi was founded at the University of Maryland on November 8th, 2017. Our chapter has been continuously growing since our founding, creating a more diverse and connected community.
