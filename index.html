About Us


Welcome to the Epsilon Chapter of Kappa Theta Pi at the University of Maryland, College Park. Kappa Theta Pi is the world’s first-co-educational professional technology fraternity. Our mission is to provide guidance to students with a shared interest in technology and build their network. Our members also come from a variety of backgrounds: computer science, engineering, business, economics, and the arts. Kappa Theta Pi UMD is open to all majors -- if you have an interest in tech, you'll fit right in!



History & Purpose


Kappa Theta Pi was originally founded at the University of Michigan in January of 2012. The Epsilon Chapter of Kappa Theta Pi was founded at the University of Maryland on November 8th, 2017. Our chapter has been continuously growing since our founding, creating a more diverse and connected community. We are proud to have cultivated an environment in which our members can grow technically, professionally, and socially.
