About Us

Welcome to the Epsilon Chapter of Kappa Theta Pi Kappa at the University of Maryland, College Park. Kappa Theta Pi is the worldâ€™s first-co-educational professional technology fraternity. Our mission is to provide guidance to students with a shared interest in technology and to cultivate an environment in which our members can grow in three major dimensions: technical, professional, and social. We seek to provide service and philanthropy to the local community by building relations within the area and with corporations.
With events throughout the year with various companies and organizations, our members are able to develop professional skills and build their network.
Our members come from a variety of backgrounds: computer science, engineering, business, economics, and the arts. Kappa Theta Pi UMD is open to all majors -- if you have an interest in tech, you'll fit right in!

History

Kappa Theta Pi was originally founded at the University of Michigan Ann Arbor in January of 2012. The Epsilon Chapter of Kappa Theta Pi was founded at the University of Maryland on November 8th, 2017. Our chapter has been continuously growing since our founding, creating a more diverse and connected community.
