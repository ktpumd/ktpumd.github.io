For Students

Any undergraduate student at the University of Maryland with an interest in technology, regardless of major, is welcome to rush Kappa Theta Pi. Kappa Theta Pi holds rush events at the beginning of the Fall and Spring semesters of each academic year. For this semester, we have concluded our rush process. However, please check back here at the beginning of the next semester to see our rush schedule. Feel free to email kappathetapiumd@gmail.com to join our email list and get reminders about our future rush events. Thank you for your interest in our Fraternity!

For Companies

Kappa Theta Pi is always looking for companies and organizations to host events with our brotherhood. If your company is interested in holding a tech talk, resume review, information session or any other kind of professional event with us, please contact us directly at kappathetapiumd@gmail.com. Thank you and we look forward to hearing from you!
