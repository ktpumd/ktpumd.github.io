About Us

We are the Epsilon chapter of Kappa Theta Pi at the University of Maryland, College Park. Kappa Theta Pi is the world's first co-educational professional technology fraternity. Our mission is to provide guidance and to cultivate an environment in which our members can grow technically, professionally, and socially.

Technical Growth

Workshops, hackathons, and hands-on projects to sharpen your skills

Brotherhood

A diverse community of like-minded individuals passionate about tech

Professional Development

Resume reviews, mock interviews, and industry connections

Where We Work

We're proud of what our members go on to achieve. From startups to industry leaders, our community continues to grow and make a meaningful impact.
